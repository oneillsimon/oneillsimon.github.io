George Conway's: A Game of Life

  • View on the Windows Store

The Game of Life is a cellular automaton devised by the British mathematician John Conway. The "game" is a zero-player game, meaning that its evolution is determined by its initial state, requiring no further input. One interacts with the Game of Life by creating an initial configuration and observing how it evolves. Developed in my 2nd year of college, this version of A Game of Life currently sits at over 1000 downloads on the Windows Phone. Unfortunately the source code seems to have become lost but if I ever retrieve it I will post it here.
